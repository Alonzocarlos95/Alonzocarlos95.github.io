Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. Each of our hotels offers a prime location in the four major cities where you can count on modern accommodations, weeding reception halls, and fine dining from our top head chefs. When you're in search of luxury with mormon flair, look to Temple Inn & Suites.
